Experience

Obtained my bachelor degree in Computer Science in 2009, then I joined a trainee program at SYDLE, a specialized I.T. company in Belo Horizonte/Brazil. As "job rotation" program I dug into the main areas of the software development process: Front-end Development, Database Administration and finally Back-end Development. At the end of the program I had to choose which area interested me more, so that's when I became a full time Software Developer.

Some years later I went to Avenue Code, a fast growing company from San Francisco, CA. There I worked for big retail clients like GAP Inc., developing complex Java systems for management of garment collections, assortments and pricing negotiation. I also participated on a internal Ruby on Rails project, ACDC, for timehseet management.

In 2014, I moved to beautiful Vancouver, BC and right away entered the payment processing industry at E-xact Transactions (and their metrics app, Telemetry). There I participated on a tough certification process, for a cool Point of Sale system, with major banks from the States. Golang, Ruby on Rails and NodeJS were just some of the tools we used.

Now I am continuing my professional quest as a Golang and C# Developer, also in the payment processing industry at PayByPhone, the leading parking payment company in Canada with strong presence in important cities like Vancouver, San Francisco, Miami and Paris.
We ♥ APIs. Most of them are developed in C#, but they invited me to spread the culture of those microsservices in Golang.

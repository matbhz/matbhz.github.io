Experience

After I obtained my bachelor degree in Computer Science in 2009, I joined a trainee program at SYDLE, a specialized I.T. company in Belo Horizonte / Brazil. In this program, I had the opportunity to dig into the main areas of the software development process: UI/UX development, Database Administration and finally Software Engineering. At the end of the program I had to choose which area interested me more, so that's when I became a full time Software Developer.

Some years later I went to Avenue Code, a fast growing company from San Francisco / CA. There I worked for big retail clients like GAP Inc., developing complex Java systems for management of collections, assortments and pricing negotiation. I also participated on a internal Ruby on Rails project, ACDC, for timehseet management.

Finally, I moved to beautiful Vancouver, BC and right away joined the payment processing industry at E-xact Transactions (and their metrics app, Telemetry). There I participated on a tough certification process, for a cool Point of Sale system, with major banks from the States. Golang, Ruby on Rails and NodeJS were just some of the tools we used.

Now I am continuing my Software Development quest as a Golang Engineer, also in the payment processing industry at PayByPhone, the leading parking payment company in Canada with strong presence in important cities like Vancouver, San Francisco, Miami and Paris, creating robust RESTful APIs in Golang (and a bit of Geoprocessing).
